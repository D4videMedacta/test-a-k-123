Why ART Certification
matters.

Not every knee surgeon is an ART surgeon. This certification represents a commitment to mastery, continuous education, and proven results.

1
Rigorous Training Requirements
Surgeons complete hundreds of supervised hours in ART-specific procedures before certification is granted.
2
Annual Recertification
ART surgeons must recertify annually, ensuring they stay current with the latest clinical protocols and outcomes data.
3
Outcomes Tracking
Every ART surgeon participates in an outcomes registry, enabling continuous quality monitoring and improvement.
4
Patient Safety Standards
Strict pre-operative and post-operative protocols minimize risk and maximize patient safety at every stage.
